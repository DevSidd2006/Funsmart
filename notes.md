# 👤 ABOUT PAGE — funsmartism.com/about

## SECTION 01 — HERO

We started FunSmartism because we kept seeing  
something schools weren't designed to notice.

Capable children who came alive in scientist-inspired environments that gave them freedom. and confused in environments that didn't. And parents who had no language for what they were seeing.  
FunSmartism Intelligence Center was built to close that gap.

---

## SECTION 02 — THE BELIEF

Three things we believe:

1. Children often don’t struggle because of marks or ability alone.  
Most struggle because they haven’t yet built strong thinking habits for unfamiliar situations.

2. Marks show outcomes. They don't show scientist-style problem-solving habits.  
A child can score well and still freeze.

3. Parents deserve more than a report card.  
A number doesn’t show how a child thinks.

---

## SECTION 03 — WHY WE BUILT THIS

We noticed it again and again: a child who struggled in a classroom would come alive the moment you put something real in their hands.

So we built a different kind of environment.  
One where thinking was the subject, not the by-product.
