# FunSmartism Intelligence Center — Homepage Layout Design

> **Design Inspiration**: STEMpedia's structure adapted for premium, observation-focused positioning  
> **Target Audience**: Parents of children 8–14 (Decision-makers, parents, educators)  
> **Tone**: Premium, calm, insightful, human

---

## 🎯 Design Principles (Inspired by STEMpedia)

✅ **What to adopt from STEMpedia:**
- Trust signal hierarchy (stats, real photos, stories from real parents)
- Multi-CTA approach throughout the page
- Section-based flow (Hero → Proof → How it works → Programs → Social proof → CTA)
- Card-based layouts for clarity
- Professional photography (real people, real environments)

❌ **What to adapt/avoid:**
- NO bright, playful colors (keep subdued, professional)
- NO tech-forward language (keep human-focused)
- NO product-centric (keep parent-outcome centric)
- Different typography hierarchy (calmer, more sophisticated)

# 🎨 VISUAL STYLE GUIDE

## Photography
- **Hero**: Real thinking lab scene (not product, not action)
- **Programs**: Authentic facilitator-child interactions (observing, genuine moments)
- **Testimonials**: Optional headshots of real parents (genuine, not stock)
- **Overall**: Warm lighting, natural poses, real environments

## Icons
- **Style**: Line-drawn, 48×56px
- **Examples**: Eye (observe), compass (approach), growth chart (resilience), lightbulb (insight)
- **Color**: Sage green (#4F8F7F) or dark (#1A1A1A)
- **Weight**: 2–2.5px stroke

## Spacing System
- Base unit: 8px
- Padding: 16px, 24px, 32px, 40px, 64px
- Margins: 32px (between sections), 16px (between elements)
- Gap (grid): 24px–32px

## Micro-interactions
- Link hover: Color shift to sage green, underline appear
- Button hover: Shadow deepen, background darken
- Card hover: Subtle lift (shadow), border highlight
- All transitions: 200ms ease
